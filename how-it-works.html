Most household and construction debris is accepted. Here's a quick reference. If you're unsure about a specific item, just call us.

✅ Allowed
Household furniture (sofas, chairs, tables)
Appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers)

General household junk and clutter

Liquids of any kind
FAQ

Process Questions Answered

Common questions about how dumpster rental works in Chicago.

From your first call to dumpster delivery can be as little as a few hours with same-day service. Getting a quote takes under 5 minutes by phone. If you call before noon, same-day delivery is available in most Chicago and suburban areas.
If the dumpster sits on your private driveway or property, no permit is required. If you need it placed on a public street or alley in Chicago, a city permit is typically required. Let us know your placement plan when you call and we'll walk you through any permit requirements.
Dumpsters must not be filled above the top rail — this is a safety and transport requirement. If a container is overfilled, the driver may not be able to pick it up until excess debris is removed, or a weight surcharge may apply. When in doubt, go up a size. We'll help you choose the right one upfront.
Yes, extensions are available. Standard rentals include 7 days. If you need more time, just let us know before your rental period ends and we'll arrange an extension. Additional daily or weekly rates apply.
Normal wear is expected and is not charged. Deposit policies vary by provider — some require one at booking, others collect on delivery. We'll explain the exact terms when we set up your rental. You won't encounter any surprises.
